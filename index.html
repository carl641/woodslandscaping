About Woods Landscaping & Dirt Work LLC

Woods Landscaping & Dirt Work LLC is owned and operated by Patrick Woods, based right here in Ardmore, Alabama. What started as a passion for the land has grown into a full-service dirt work, landscaping, and construction support business serving Limestone County and the surrounding areas.

We take pride in every job — whether it's grading a yard, clearing a lot, laying sod, or running water lines. We treat every property like it's our own and every customer like family.
