From Bare Dirt to a Beautiful Yard

After the grading is done and the construction dust settles, your yard still needs that final step to really look like home. That's where our lawn and landscaping services come in. Woods Landscaping & Dirt Work LLC handles everything from laying fresh sod to spreading seed and straw, building out flower beds, and applying mulch — all the finishing work that gives your property real curb appeal.

We do a lot of lawn restoration work for homeowners who've had construction done on their property, whether it's a new home build, an addition, or utility work that tore up the yard. We grade the area smooth, bring in topsoil if needed, and then lay sod or seed depending on your budget and timeline. Sod gives you an instant lawn, while seed and straw is a more affordable option that fills in nicely over a few weeks with proper watering. Either way, we make sure the ground underneath is right so your grass takes hold and stays healthy.

For folks who want their landscaping to look a little more polished, we also install flower beds, edge them out clean, and fill them with fresh mulch. Top dressing existing lawns with a thin layer of quality soil is another service we offer — it helps fill in low spots, improves soil quality, and gives your grass a boost heading into the growing season. It's the kind of detail work that makes the difference between a yard that looks okay and one that looks great.

What We Offer

  • Sod installation for instant, green lawns
  • Seed and straw application for new or patchy yards
  • Top dressing with quality topsoil
  • Flower bed creation and edging
  • Mulch delivery and installation
  • Lawn restoration after construction or utility work
  • Topsoil hauling and spreading
  • Final grading and smoothing before sod or seed
