Take Back Your Property

Overgrown lots, tangled fence lines, and thick brush can turn a great piece of property into a real mess. Whether you've got a few acres that have gotten away from you or you're preparing a lot for new construction, Woods Landscaping & Dirt Work LLC has the equipment to clear it out and get your land looking the way it should.

We handle everything from small residential cleanups to larger lot clearing jobs. Our skid steer, tractor with bush hog, and mini excavator let us tackle brush, small trees, stumps, and undergrowth efficiently. If you've got bamboo taking over your property — and around here, that's more common than most folks realize — we can root it out and help you reclaim that ground.

Fence lines and tree lines tend to get neglected over the years. Vines, saplings, and brush creep in and before you know it, you can't even see your fence anymore. We clean all of that up, clearing out the overgrowth so your property looks maintained and your fence line is accessible again. It's the kind of work that makes a big difference in how your place looks and how easy it is to manage.

What We Offer

  • Bush hog service for fields and overgrown lots
  • Fence line and tree line cleanup
  • Bamboo removal and root clearing
  • Brush clearing and debris hauling
  • Lot clearing for new home construction
  • Stump removal and grinding
  • Post-clearing grading and leveling
