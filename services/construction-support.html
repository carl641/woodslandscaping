Your Partner on the Job Site

Building a new home or adding onto your property takes a lot of coordination, and the dirt work is one of the most important pieces of the puzzle. At Woods Landscaping, we work alongside builders, general contractors, and homeowners to handle the site prep, grading, and finish work that every construction project needs. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and get the job done so your project stays on schedule.

Whether you need a lot cleared and graded before the foundation crew arrives, a driveway cut in, or a pool pad leveled and compacted, we have the equipment to handle it. Our skid steer, mini excavator, dozer, and dump truck give us the versatility to take on jobs of all sizes — from small residential additions to full new construction sites.

After the build is done, we come back for the finish work too. Final grading, spreading topsoil, and getting the yard ready for sod or seed are all part of what we do. We understand that the yard is one of the last things a homeowner sees before they move in, and we make sure it looks great.

What We Offer

  • New construction yard prep and finish
  • Driveway installation and grading
  • Demolition and debris hauling
  • Pool pad preparation and leveling
  • Site preparation and rough grading
  • Final grade with topsoil spreading
  • Backfill around foundations and utilities
  • Material delivery to job sites
