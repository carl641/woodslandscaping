Material Delivery & Installation

A good driveway, parking pad, or work area starts with the right material laid down properly. At Woods Landscaping, we haul and spread a wide range of surface materials using our dump truck and skid steer. Whether you need a load of gravel to fix up your driveway, crushed concrete for a parking area, or sand and fill dirt for a project you're working on, we can get it to your property and put it exactly where it needs to go.

Around Limestone County, a lot of driveways and access roads take a beating — especially after a wet winter or heavy equipment traffic. Potholes form, material washes out, and before long you're dodging ruts every time you pull in. We can resurface your existing driveway with fresh gravel or slag, grade it properly so water runs off the edges instead of pooling in the middle, and get it back to where it should be. It's one of those jobs that makes a noticeable difference the moment we're done.

We also prep parking pads for shops, outbuildings, RVs, and equipment storage. That means grading the base, compacting the soil, and laying the right material at the right depth so it holds up under weight and weather. If you're not sure which material is best for your situation, just give us a call and we'll walk you through the options. We've been doing this long enough to know what works and what doesn't in our Alabama clay.
