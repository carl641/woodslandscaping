Underground Work You Can Trust

Running water lines, trenching for utilities, and working around existing underground infrastructure requires precision and care. At Woods Landscaping & Dirt Work LLC, we use our skid steer and mini excavator to dig clean, accurate trenches while being mindful of what's already in the ground. Whether you're running a new water line out to a barn, installing a frost-free hydrant, or need trenching for electrical or plumbing, we handle it with the attention it deserves.

A lot of property owners in Limestone County need utility work done but aren't sure where to start. Maybe you bought a piece of land and need a water line run from the road to your build site. Maybe your old water line is leaking and needs to be replaced. Or maybe you're adding an outbuilding and need utilities brought to it. Whatever the situation, we can dig the trench, lay the PVC, and backfill everything clean so you'd never know we were there.

We also provide support for septic line work, coordinating with licensed plumbers and septic installers to handle the digging and backfill portions of the job. Our equipment lets us work in tight spaces and around existing landscaping without tearing up more of your yard than necessary. We take our time and do it right the first time.

What We Offer

  • Water line installation and replacement
  • Trenching with skid steer and mini excavator
  • Frost-free hydrant installation
  • PVC pipe supply and installation
  • Utility trenching for electrical and plumbing
  • Septic line digging and backfill support
  • Careful excavation around existing utilities
  • Clean backfill and site restoration after trenching
