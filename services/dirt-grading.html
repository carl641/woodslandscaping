Professional Dirt Work & Grading

Whether you're building a new home, fixing drainage problems, or just trying to get your yard back in shape, proper grading is where it all starts. At Woods Landscaping & Dirt Work LLC, we use our skid steer, mini excavator, and dozer to move dirt the right way — so water flows where it should and your property stays solid for years to come.

A lot of folks around Limestone County deal with standing water in their yard, erosion along ditches, or uneven ground that makes mowing a headache. We see it all the time, and we know how to fix it. Good grading isn't just about moving dirt around — it's about reading the lay of the land and making sure everything drains properly away from your home and outbuildings.

We also work with builders on new construction sites, handling everything from rough grading before the foundation goes in to final grade after the house is finished. If your yard got torn up during construction or you've got a lot that needs leveling before you build, we've got the equipment and the experience to get it done right.

What We Offer

  • Yard grading for proper drainage and a level lawn
  • Ditch cleanout and reshaping to prevent flooding
  • Soil conditioning to improve ground quality
  • Terrace leveling for sloped properties
  • Lot leveling for new construction or landscaping
  • Drainage solutions to redirect standing water
  • Fill dirt hauling and spreading
  • Rough and final grading for builders
