Project Overview

In March 2026, we tackled a land leveling project that wrapped up with a custom fire pit — turning an uneven yard into a smooth, usable space with a great spot to gather around in the evenings.

We started by bringing in the equipment to knock down the high spots, fill in the low ones, and grade the yard out flat. Getting the ground level not only makes the property look better, it also helps with drainage and gives the homeowners a yard they can actually use without tripping over dips and humps.

Once the leveling was dialed in, we built out the fire pit area — a clean, simple setup that gives the yard a focal point and a place for the family to enjoy. End to end, the property went from rough and uneven to leveled out and ready to enjoy.

What We Did

  • Yard leveling and grading
  • Cut high spots, filled low spots
  • Smoothed and finished topsoil
  • Custom fire pit installation
  • Final cleanup
