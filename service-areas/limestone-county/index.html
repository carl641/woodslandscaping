Landscaping & Dirt Work in Limestone County

Woods Landscaping is based right here in Ardmore, Alabama, in the heart of Limestone County. This is where we live, where we raise our families, and where we've built our reputation one job at a time. From the rolling farmland south of Elkmont to the growing neighborhoods around Athens, we know this county inside and out — the soil, the terrain, the drainage patterns, and the challenges that come with building and maintaining property in North Alabama.

Whether you need a yard graded for a new home site, a fence line cleared on a rural property, a gravel driveway refreshed before winter, or sod laid to finish off a construction project, we bring the right equipment and the experience to get it done right. We serve homeowners, contractors, farmers, and commercial property owners throughout Limestone County, including Ardmore, Athens, Elkmont, Mooresville, Belle Mina, Lester, Tanner, and everywhere in between.

Limestone County is growing fast, especially around Athens and the Highway 72 corridor. New subdivisions, commercial sites, and rural properties all need dependable dirt work and landscaping support. As a local, fully licensed and insured business, we take pride in being the crew our neighbors trust to handle their land right.
