Landscaping & Dirt Work in Athens, Alabama

Athens is the county seat and largest city in Limestone County, and it's one of the areas we work in most. Located just about 15 minutes south of our home base in Ardmore along US-31, Athens is a quick trip for our crew and equipment. We know the neighborhoods, the new developments along Highway 72 and Lindsay Lane, and the older established areas near the historic downtown square.

Athens has seen tremendous growth in recent years, driven by new residential subdivisions, commercial development, and its proximity to Huntsville and Redstone Arsenal. That growth means a steady demand for new construction yard prep, grading, land clearing, and sod installation. Whether it's getting a freshly built lot ready for landscaping or clearing an overgrown parcel for a new build, we handle the heavy lifting so contractors and homeowners can move forward on schedule.

Beyond new construction, Athens homeowners regularly call us for driveway resurfacing with gravel or crushed concrete, ditch cleanout after heavy rains, yard regrading to fix drainage problems, and bush hogging on larger rural lots on the outskirts of town. If you have a project in Athens or the surrounding area, give Patrick a call — we're practically right around the corner.
