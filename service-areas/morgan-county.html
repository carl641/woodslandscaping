Landscaping & Dirt Work in Morgan County

Morgan County sits just south of our home base in Limestone County, and it's an area we work in regularly. From Decatur along the Tennessee River to Hartselle, Falkville, Somerville, and the rural communities throughout the county, Woods Landscaping is ready and willing to travel to get the job done. We bring the same equipment, the same quality, and the same fair pricing that our Limestone County customers have come to expect.

Morgan County offers a mix of terrain and project types that keeps our crew busy. Along the river in Decatur and the Point Mallard area, we handle grading and drainage work for waterfront and near-water properties where proper soil management is critical. In Hartselle and the communities along I-65, new residential growth means demand for construction site prep, lot clearing, driveway installation, and sod work. Out in the more rural parts of the county, we're often called for bush hogging, fence line clearing, and gravel road maintenance.

We understand that hiring a contractor from a neighboring county can raise questions, so let us put your mind at ease: Woods Landscaping is fully licensed and insured, and we treat every Morgan County job with the same care and attention as if it were right next door. Most locations in Morgan County are within 30 to 45 minutes of our shop in Ardmore, so mobilization is quick and efficient.
